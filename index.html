✦ Questions we hear all the time

You have questions.
I have answers.

Do I need any electronics knowledge?
+
Absolutely not. Zero. We built Varible specifically for people who have a cool idea but no idea how to make it happen. You describe what you want in plain everyday English — we handle everything techy.
What even is an Arduino or ESP32?
+
Think of them as tiny, cheap computers about the size of a credit card. You can program them to control lights, motors, sensors, displays, or pretty much anything. They're the brain inside most DIY electronics projects.
How much does a custom build cost?
+
It depends on the complexity. Simple projects (a sensor, a display, a buzzer) can be very affordable. We always give you a clear price quote before any work begins — no surprises. During beta, we're offering special discounted rates.
What if my idea is really vague?
+
Perfect, honestly. "I want something that turns my fan on automatically when it gets hot" is plenty to work with. We'll ask you questions and help shape the idea together. The rougher the idea, the more fun we have figuring it out.
What's the Edu Kit exactly?
+
It's a box we mail you containing every single part you need to build a specific electronics project. You choose the project (like a weather station or robot), we pack the components, and you build it step by step using our online guide. It's designed for total beginners.
What does "beta testing" mean?
+
It means we're still early — we're working with our first real customers to make sure everything works great before we launch publicly on Kickstarter. Beta users get special access, lower prices, and direct contact with us. If you join beta, you're helping shape the final product.
