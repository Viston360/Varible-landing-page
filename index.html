✦ The process

What happens after
you message us?

Simple, friendly, and no techy gatekeeping. Here's exactly what happens from start to finish.

01

You tell us what you want

Write it like you'd explain it to a friend. "I want something that waters my plants automatically" is a perfect brief. We take it from there.

02

We figure out how to build it

We pick the right tiny computer, design the circuit, and write up a simple plan. We share it with you first and only proceed when you're happy.

03

We build it and show you it working

We solder, program, and test everything. Before it ships, we send you a short video of your actual device working. You'll see it in action.

04

It arrives, ready to use

Your device shows up with a simple guide and all the code. And we stay available — got questions later? Just message us. We're here.
